Spencer Nettles' User Manual

My Style

* I like to do things on my own and then seek help and advice when needed

* I recharge by being alone with my thoughts or with a few of my closest friends. This allows me to recharge and think clearly through the situations and problems in my life

* The best way to do things is to really think about it first and come up with a solid plan. When that plan is made then execute it.

* My attention to detail is what makes me stand out in the work place. I like to find the balance between quakity and quantity by doing a realy good job but not taking forever to do it.

How best to communicate with me

* If its in a email or document I preffer if it was short and sweet. Tell me what I need to know in the fastest way possible. This helps me know what you want and how I can help

* Approach me as a friend. I like talking to my friends and value their input, so approach me as someone you respect and aren't afraid to disagree with in a respectful way.

* Don't interupt when im busy with something. When you want to talk just ask. If its a good time, I would love to talk to you. But if I am deep in a project and cant loose my train of thought I'll tell you thats its not right now and when I get a break I'll come to you.

What I value

* Honesty, I value honesty and when people take responsibility for their actions. This shows to me that they are ready to move forward and work hard.

* Trust, When people trust me and I trust them we have a great work relationship. I can focus on my work and now worry about theirs and vice versa. This is also why I value honesty because that's what builds my trust in someone.

* Effeciency, I value people that can do a good job and get teh job done fast. It makes the work we do go by so much smoother and faster. Thats why I try and be as effecient as possible.

What people misunderstand about me

I tend to make decisions based on feeling and less logic. I tend to trust my gut feeling, but this can be a good or bad thing. This can lead to decisions I want rather than whats best. I have been trying to be more logical with my decision making but I tend to get hooked on one thing and try and learn all I can about it and then do it until something else catches my attention and I repeat the cycle.

How to help me

* Let me know if I am wrong. I make plenty of mistakes so if you catch one let me know.

* If you dont aggree with what I am doing or the ideas I have let me know. I realy respect when people give ideas or oppinions contrary to the higher ups. If they do it in a respectful way it shows they truley care about the job and don't just suck up to managment.

* Be patient with me. Sometimes it takes a while for me to understand a concept you are trying to convey but when I get it, I get it. So don't get frustrated if I don't understand what you are trying to say right away.

What I don't have patience for

* Lying, I don't have patience for lying. It ruins trust and shows me who is and isn't honest. I don't always like to rely on other but when I need to I will always choose those I think are trustworthy.

* Sloppy workers, I want a job to be done right in the work force so if people do a bad job I will rather send someone that does a good job. Unless they are learning and trying. I always respect those that are trying to imrpove their craft because it shows dedication.
